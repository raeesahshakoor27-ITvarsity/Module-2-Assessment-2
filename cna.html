CNA

CNA has 168 stores countrywide and has embarked on a 5 year growth plan focussed on smaller store/shop sizes in convenient locations to better serve the broader South African customer.

We continue with our strategy of being your favourite stationery store. Stationery, our category killer dominates in our stores with a full spectrum of stationery across scholastic, home office and fashionable on trend items. In addition, there is a renewed focus in our Arts and Crafts ranges with a dramatically improved and increased offer to our customers.

Our secondary strategic focus remains Education. While we currently dominate the market with our study guides, the team is currently working on a comprehensive ECD (Education Child Development) range across the various life and learning stages of children with the involvement of Occupational Therapists and other subject matter experts in the industry.
